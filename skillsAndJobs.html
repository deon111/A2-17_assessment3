UI Designer

Brief:

A successful UI designer will have a great eye for design, using the latest techniques and trends to deliver top notch prototypes, ready for development. Having knowledge of Adobe XD and basic front-end coding languages will be paramount for creating these prototypes. Confidence and being able to work well within a team is vital as you will need to interact with team members, as well as presenting designs and prototypes.

    Responsibilities:
  • Collaborating visuals, product direction and experience with engineers and developers.
  • Implementing unique, simple and user-friendly ideas to overcome design issues.
  • Creating Story boards, wireframes, user and process flows to communicate design ideas.
  • Present designs and key prototypes to peers and clientele.
  • Conduct and evaluate user research.
    Required Skills and Qualifications:
  • Creativity with design and problem solving
  • JavaScript, CSS, HTML
  • Proficiency with Adobe XD
  • Visual design skills
  • Bachelor of UI design
  • Minimum one-year UI designer experience
  • Stong portfolio
  • Confident presentation skills

Data Scientist

Brief:

A data scientist is required to research and develop new Question Prompt Lists (QPLs) for different treatments, illnesses and contexts. A secondary role is to support the app’s user experience and business plans and strategies by analysing industry data. The ideal candidate is skilled at analysing large data sets and using models to improve business decisions, product and process optimization. The individual must be experienced in using various data analysis/data mining methods and tools, creating and implementing models and algorithms and running simulations. Proof of contributing to business through data-based insights is required. Strong presentation skills and effective communication of information to a range of stakeholders and functional teams is required. The individual must be able effectively work in a team and individually. Experience working as a data scientist in the healthcare industry for more than a year is preferred.

    Responsibilities:
  • Analyse and mine company and industry data for the development of QPLs and improvement of general product development and business strategies
  • Work with team and stakeholders to identify business opportunities and solutions by leveraging industry and company data
  • Carry out tests for products to evaluate user experience and product functionality and effectiveness
  • Assess data gathering techniques, data sources and company test data for accuracy and effectiveness
  • Develop customised data algorithms and models to use on data
  • Assess and develop tools to ensure quality data algorithm and model performance
  • Develop new QPLs and improve old QPLs
  • Apply predictive modelling to optimize customer experience, ad targeting, sales and other business outcomes
  • Coordinate with functional teams to monitor outcomes, implement models and communicate relevant data
  • Effectively and efficiently present relevant data to stakeholders
    Required Skills and Qualifications:
  • Creative and strong problem solving skills for product development and business strategies and processes
  • Knowledge of and experience with querying databases several statistical computer languages (R, Python, SLQ, etc.) for application to data sets
  • Knowledge of and experience with web services (Redshift, S3, Digital Ocean, Spark, etc.) and several coding languages (C, C++, Java, Java Script, etc.)
  • Knowledge of and experience with data mining and statistical techniques (GLM/Regression, Boosting, Random Forest, social network analysis, text mining, etc.)
  • Knowledge of and experience with analysing and manipulating data from third-party providers (Coremetrics, Google Analytics, Adwords, Site Catalyst, Facebook Insights, Crimson Hexagon, etc.)
  • Knowledge of and experience with creating and using data architectures
  • Knowledge of and experience with machine learning techniques (artificial neural networks, clustering, decision tree learning, etc.)
  • Teamwork skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills for communicating and cohesively working with teams and stakeholders
  • Knowledge of and experience with distributed data tools (MySQL, Hive, Hadoop, Gurobi, Spark, Map/Reduce, etc.)
  • Knowledge of and experience with visualising data using computing tools (ggplot, Business Objects, D3, Periscope, etc.)
  • Passion to learn new technology and techniques
  • Time management and task organization skills
  • Master’s or PHD in Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or related fields
  • (Preferred) A year of work experience as a data scientist in the healthcare industry

IT Analyst

Brief:

The IT analyst will work in business management and help determine the IT and business objectives for improving business outcomes. This role will require more work in the business management role than a typical IT analyst is used to. This is to help the lack of business skills and experience of the current team. The individual will design and implement data systems for optimizing managerial efficiencies. The role involves trouble shooting systems and keeping company updated on the best and latest practices with IT standards and guidelines. The ideal candidate will be skilled in troubleshooting systems, software installation and database programming. The candidate must have work experience in the healthcare industry’s business management department for a minimum of three years. The analyst must be able to effectively work and communicate with the IT staff, non-technical staff and stakeholders.

    Responsibilities:
  • Analyse business and IT requirements and provide objective advice on the use of IT and business management strategies and practices
  • Effectively and efficiently communicate relevant information to stakeholders and functional teams
  • Install, update and configure hardware and software components
  • Test and assess new technology, software and hardware
  • Troubleshoot software and hardware problems
    Required Skills and Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in IT, computer science or engineering or related field
  • 3 years of minimum work experience in the healthcare industry’s business management department
  • Knowledge of and experience with troubleshooting systems
  • Knowledge of and experience with database programming and software installation (Preferred) A+ and Linux+ Certification
  • Knowledge or and experience with various software and hardware
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Knowledge of and experience with business management practices and strategies
  • Time management and task organisation skills
  • Teamwork skills

Information Security Analyst

Brief:

The Information security analyst will design and apply IT security systems to safeguard the organization’s computer networks and cloud computing environments. The individual will aid in developing best practices for the company’s IT security. The analyst will monitor security breaches for computer networks, install security software and document security problems. The analyst should be aware of cybersecurity trends, update security measures and will be responsible for meeting compliances. An ideal candidate will have a minimum 3 years of work experience in information or cyber security. Experienced in penetration testing and techniques, access management, identity solutions, cloud computing security audits, documenting security issues and installing security software. The analyst should have excellent communication and teamwork skills and can effectively work alone.

    Responsibilities:
  • Security audits for cloud servers
  • Operate software and install security measures and antivirus software
  • Responsible for access management and identity management solutions
  • Upgrade systems to enable security software and meet security compliances
  • Analyse security requirements and give advice for IT security requirements
  • Test and assess new technology
  • Perform penetration testing and cyberattacks
  • Design and implement effective IT security systems
  • Gather and analyse feedback from end-users for improving systems
    Required Skills and Qualifications:
  • (Preferred) MBA in Information Systems
  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or Computer Science
  • Minimum 3 years of work experience in information security
  • CISA, CCSK and CISSP certifications
  • Excellent verbal and written skills for effective communication with functional teams and stakeholders or management
  • Knowledge of and experience with penetration techniques and testing
  • Knowledge of and experience with identifying and mitigating network and cloud computing vulnerabilities
  • Knowledge of patch management
  • Knowledge of and experience with IDPS concepts, firewalls and antivirus
  • Knowledge of and experience with documenting security problems and installing security software
  • Teamwork skills
  • Time management and task organization skills
